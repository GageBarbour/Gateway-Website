About our home away from home

We are experienced in saving companies money on housing their crews as well as individuals who are working on various projects in the area. We strive to provide a lodging experience that provides the comforts and privacy of home. Making us your home away from home.

Rent a room, rent one floor, or rent the whole house. We are completely flexible with witch rooms we rent out. We are able to rent out single rooms, clear up to the whole house. Rates include all bills, cable, and high-speed internet. Best part with all of this, there is no hassle with long term contracts!

Every room includes a cable jack, and fully furnished bedroom set. Each floor has its own kitchen and its own separate entrance. There is also a washer and dryer* on the premises, with a few exceptions. We also have connections with a local cleaning service that for an extra fee will clean for $17.50/hour. No bedrooms share walls and every room has its own door. We also have a lot in the back that allows for 8 trucks to be parked.

On top of all these great features we also have long term discounts. After six months we reduce rent by 15%. And if you decide to stay with us for over a year we will drop down to 30%! This decrease on rent is one of the many ways we save companies on lodging.

* The washer and dryer are only available when the entire first floor isn't rented out to single group.
