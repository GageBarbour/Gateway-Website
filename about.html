About our home away from home

The Gateway house features two floors of furnished living space.

The main floor has XX bedrooms, XX bathrooms, living room, and kitchen.

The top floor contains 4 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms, a living room and kitchen.

Every room includes a cablejack and each floor has its own separate entrance. There is also a washer and dryer* on the premises. We have a contract with a local cleaning service that will clean your space for an extra fee of $17.50/hour. No bedrooms share wall and we also have a lot in the back that allows for up to 8 trucks to be parked.

Rates include all bills, cable, and highspeed internet. The best part is that there are no hassles with long term contracts. You decide how long you will stay based on your individual needs.

We also offer long term stay discounts. After four continuous months we will reduce the rent by 10%. And if you stay with us for over eight months we will reduce rent by 15%. This decrease on rent is one of the many ways we help save companies and individuals on lodging.

* The washer and dryer are only available when the entire first floor isn't rented out to single group.
